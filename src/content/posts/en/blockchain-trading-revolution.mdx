The prop trading industry has long been plagued by opacity, hidden fees, and questionable practices. Blockchain technology is changing everything, bringing unprecedented transparency and fairness to proprietary trading.

## The Problem with Traditional Prop Firms

### Hidden Agenda
Traditional prop firms often profit more from evaluation fees than from traders' success. This creates a misaligned incentive structure where firms benefit from trader failures.

### Lack of Transparency
- Unclear profit splits
- Hidden fees and charges
- Opaque trading conditions
- No way to verify firm's claims

### Payment Delays
Withdrawals can take weeks or months, with firms finding reasons to delay or deny payouts.
