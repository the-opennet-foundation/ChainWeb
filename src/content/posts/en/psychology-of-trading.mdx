## The Mental Game

Trading is 90% psychology and 10% strategy. Even the best trading system will fail if you can't control your emotions and stick to your plan.

### Common Psychological Pitfalls

- **Fear of Missing Out (FOMO)** - Jumping into trades without proper analysis
- **Revenge Trading** - Making impulsive trades to recover losses
- **Overconfidence** - Taking excessive risks after a winning streak
- **Analysis Paralysis** - Overthinking and missing opportunities
- **Loss Aversion** - Holding losing positions too long

## Building Mental Resilience

### 1. Accept Losses as Part of Trading

No trader wins 100% of the time. The key is maintaining a positive risk-reward ratio and cutting losses quickly.

### 2. Develop a Pre-Trade Routine

Create a checklist before every trade:
- Is this trade part of my strategy?
- What's my entry, stop-loss, and target?
- What's my risk per trade?
- Am I emotionally ready to take this trade?

### 3. Keep a Trading Journal

Document every trade with:
- Entry and exit reasons
- Emotional state during the trade
- What you learned
- Areas for improvement

### 4. Practice Mindfulness

Meditation and breathing exercises help maintain emotional control during volatile markets.
